Gulab jamun

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Khoya
  • Ghee
  • Flour

recipe

  1. Step 1: Boil sugar and water to prepare syrup remove from fire. Add cardamom and keep aside to cool.
  2. Step 2: In a mixing bowl put the flour, paneer, sooji, Nestlé MILKMAID, baking powder and baking soda. Mix gently to make a soft dough. Do not knead too much.
  3. Step 3: Divide the mixture into 30-35 portions and gently roll into round gulab jamuns. Fry in the oil at very low flame till golden brown in color.
  4. Step 4: Keep putting the fried gulab jamuns in the prepared sugar syrup. Once all the gulab jamuns are in the sugar syrup bring it to a boil and remove from flame
  5. Step 5: Serve warm

Soan papdi

Ingredients

  • gramflour
  • plain flour (maida)
  • Ghee
  • sugar
  • milk
  • cardamom seeds crushed coarsely

recipe

  1. Heat ghee in a heavy saucepan.
  2. Add flour mixture and roast on low till light golden.
  3. Make syrup out of sugar, water and milk
  4. Pour syrup at once into the flour mixture.
  5. Beat well with a large fork till the mixture forms threadlike flakes.
  6. Pour onto a greased surface or thali and roll to 1" thickness lightly.
  7. Sprinkle the charmagaz seeds and elaichi and gently press down with palm.
  8. Cool, cut into 1" squares, wrap individually into square pieces of thin plastic sheet.
